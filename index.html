B.A. Economics and Computer Science


Hi, my name is Jeremy Seow and I am a senior at Claremont McKenna College, studying Computer Science and Economics with a concentration in finance.

I’m interested in the intersection of finance and technology - specifically the disruption and subsequent value created through different technology sectors. Over the past few years, I’ve developed my financial skills through internships at two private equity firms and through my coursework. I have also gained insights into small and large technology companies - having worked at Redmart and Uber. I am currently pursuing a career in finance - a path that started from my first dream job being an inventor.

Outside of academics and my career, I enjoy working out and keeping fit. I’ve played rugby since I was 8 and have won two small college National Championships. I love to cook and bake - and started a club at college named Let’s Spoon Cooking and Baking Club. I also enjoy playing basketball, gardening, scuba diving, and snowboarding.

My current projects are: applying for a job in finance, creating a social food connector application, and co-starting a podcast aimed at empowering survivors to take control of their lives.
